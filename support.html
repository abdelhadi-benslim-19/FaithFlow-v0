Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I reset my password?
    You can reset your password by going to the account settings and clicking "Reset Password."
  • How do I update my profile information?
    You can update your profile information in the account settings under "Edit Profile."
  • How do I add a new widget?
    Navigate to the Widgets page and select the widget you want to add to your home screen.
  • How do I track my daily prayers?
    Use the Prayer Tracker widget available on the Prayer page or the Widgets page.
